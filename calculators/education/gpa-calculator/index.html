How GPA is Calculated

Your GPA (Grade Point Average) is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credits taken.

Formula:

GPA = (Sum of (Grade Points × Course Credits)) / Total Credits

Disclaimer:

This GPA calculator is based on the 4.0 grading scale. Different schools may use different scales or weighting systems.
