Study Time Calculator

How Study Time is Calculated

Based on the number of credit hours and study intensity, this calculator estimates how many hours per week you should dedicate to each course.

Formula:

Total Hours = Σ (credit hours × intensity multiplier)

Disclaimer:

This is a general guide. Actual study needs may vary depending on course difficulty, personal learning speed, and goals.
