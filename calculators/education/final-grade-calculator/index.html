Final Grade Calculator

How Final Exam Grade is Calculated

This tool uses the weighted average formula to calculate what grade you need on your final exam to reach your desired course grade.

Formula:

Final Exam Score = (Desired Grade - (1 - Final Weight) × Current Grade) / Final Weight

Disclaimer:

This calculator uses standard percentage math. Your institution may round differently or include other graded components.
