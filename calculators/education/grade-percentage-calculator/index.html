Grade Percentage Calculator

How Grade Percentage is Calculated

The grade percentage is a measure of your performance, calculated by dividing the points you earned by the total possible points and multiplying by 100.

Formula:

Percentage = (Points Earned / Total Points) * 100

Example:

If you earned 18 out of 20 points:
(18 / 20) * 100 = 90%

Disclaimer:

This calculator gives a basic percentage. Your final grade may depend on weighted averages or grading scales used by your instructor.
