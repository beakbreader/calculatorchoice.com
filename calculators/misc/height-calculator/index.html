How This Height Predictor Works

This tool combines the Mid-Parental Height Method with a growth adjustment based on your current age and height. While genetics play a major role, nutrition, sleep, and lifestyle can also affect final adult height.

Typical predictions are accurate within 5 cm. For children under 10, this tool gives a more generalized estimate. For teens, it becomes increasingly accurate as the body nears maturity.
