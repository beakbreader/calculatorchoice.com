How Baby Feeding Estimates Work

Infants typically need about 150 ml of milk per kg of body weight per day, or about 2.5 oz per pound.

Formulas:

  • Daily intake (ml) = weight (kg) × 150
  • Daily intake (oz) = weight (lb) × 2.5

Disclaimer:

This calculator provides an estimate. Always consult a pediatrician for feeding guidelines tailored to your baby’s specific needs.
