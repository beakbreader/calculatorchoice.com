Understanding Baby Growth Charts

This calculator estimates where your baby's measurements might fall compared to WHO growth charts.

How It Works:

  • Calculates rough percentiles for weight, length, and head size
  • Uses gender-specific age bands and median-based logic

Disclaimer:

This is not a clinical tool. For precise percentile rankings and guidance, consult a pediatrician or use official WHO/CDC charts.
