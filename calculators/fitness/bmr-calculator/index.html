BMR Calculator

About BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) represents the number of calories your body burns at rest. It helps estimate your daily energy needs.

Formula:

Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:

Men: 10×weight + 6.25×height − 5×age + 5
Women: 10×weight + 6.25×height − 5×age − 161

Disclaimer:

Results provide an estimate of calories burned per day at rest. Actual needs vary with activity and individual factors.
