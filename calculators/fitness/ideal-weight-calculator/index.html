Ideal Weight Calculator

How Ideal Weight is Determined

This calculator uses the Devine formula to estimate a healthy weight range based on your height and gender.

Formula:

Men: 50 kg + 2.3 kg × (height in inches − 60)
Women: 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg × (height in inches − 60)

Disclaimer:

Ideal weight formulas provide general guidance and do not account for individual differences in body composition.
