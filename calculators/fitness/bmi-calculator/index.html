    What is BMI?

    Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height. It helps you understand if you're underweight, within a healthy weight range, overweight, or obese. Our BMI calculator gives you instant results based on the standard BMI formula used worldwide.

    Results:

    • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
    • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
    • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
    • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
