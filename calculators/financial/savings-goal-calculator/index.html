How to Reach Your Savings Goal

Whether you're saving for a home, a car, or a vacation, understanding how much to save each month helps you stay on track. This calculator factors in interest earned on your savings.

Formula

PMT = FV Ã— (r / ((1 + r)^nt - 1))
Where:
FV = future value (goal amount)
r = monthly interest rate
t = number of years
n = 12 (monthly contributions)

Example

To save $10,000 in 5 years at a 3% annual interest rate, you need to save approximately $152.15 per month.
